About Us

Warteg Sederhana has been serving authentic Indonesian cuisine since 1995. Our recipes have been passed down through generations, bringing you the true taste of Indonesian home cooking.

We pride ourselves on using fresh, locally-sourced ingredients and traditional cooking methods to ensure the best quality food for our customers.
